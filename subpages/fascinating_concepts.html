Fascinating Concepts

These are some of the concepts that I found intriguing. It is always interesting to ask new people what concepts fascinate them the most. I always learn something new. Every person has their own list. Here is mine. See if you are familiar with all of them. If not, I do recommend checking them out. Let me know if I have missed something. :-)

  • Euler's identity
  • Everything about calculus
  • Principle of conservation of momentum
  • Gauss divergence theorem
  • Fourier series and Transforms, the FFT algorithm.
  • Eigen value analysis
  • Mohr's circle - How a piece of chalk breaks under tension and torsion
  • Gyroscopes and Bicycles
  • Coriolis effect
  • Castigliano's Theorems
  • Cauchy's integral theorem
  • Planck's black body radiation
  • Einstein's 1905 papers (Special relativity, Photo-electric effect, Brownian-motion)
  • Uncertainty principle
  • Principle of equivalence
  • Young's double slit experiment
  • Superfluid helium
  • Cryogenic liquefaction cycles
  • Monte Carlo method
  • Modal superposition technique
  • Fermat's last theorem
  • Michelson - Morley experiment
  • Neural Networks
  • Absorption of sound in porous media
